The Creed of One

Book One of the Assassin's Notebook

This is not your typical sword and spell fantasy novel. This book will rope the reader in when the hero deals with women, brothels, zombies, cannibals, thieves, thugs, guns, cults, torture, demons, all forms of magic, and much more.

In the decaying city of Los Demos, a skilled assassin who is known only by his countless aliases must find out who has put out a contract to assassinate him and why. In the process, he gets tangled in a web of mysteries and learns the fate of the world depends on him.
